Recap of Flash Memory Summit 2023

Driving Innovation in Flash Storage with Silicon Motion!

The highly anticipated Flash Memory Summit 2023, held from August 8 to August 10, 2023, at Silicon Motion Booth #315, the event showcased Silicon Motion's latest new PCIe Gen5 SSD controller platform for Enterprise Servers and Datacenter as well as the world's first PCIe Gen4 SSD controller with Single Root-IO Virtualization (SR-IOV) for Automotive and we also unveil the upcoming PCIe Gen5 controller for PC SSDs.

Here, we wanted to share this recap to provide you with our latest technologies and the experts' insight.

  • MonTitan™ PCIe Gen5 Enterprise SSD Development Platform
  • The World's First PCIe Gen4 SSD Controller with SR-IOV for Automotive
  • The PCIe NVMe SSD Controller Solution for Personal Computers
